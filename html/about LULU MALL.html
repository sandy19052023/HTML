LuLu Mall. The World of Happiness.

  • LuLu Mall Thiruvananthapuram is a division of the much-famed LuLu Group, headquartered in Abu Dhabi UAE. It is one of the largest as well as the first of its kind in India. Located at Akkulam, on NH 66 bypass, in close proximity to Technopark, LuLu Mall will open its doors for an enchanting international shopping

  • With a built-up space of 185800 sq. m., the mall is spread across ground plus two storeys. The mall has over 300 national and international brands and 12 Anchor Stores including the LuLu Hypermarket spread across 18500 sq.

  • Funtura, at 7400 sq. m. is the largest indoor entertainment zone in the country. Its biggest attractions include a nearly 1000 ft long roll glider which circulates the food court, wall climbing for adults and kids, 6 lane bowling alleys, 550 sq. m. trampoline, Tag Arena, 7D theatre with interactive games, roller coaster, arcade games with VR zone, carousel and much more.

  • The Mall will also feature a 2500-seater food court, as well as several restaurants and cafes, that serve various regional, international and speciality cuisines. With ample parking for over 3800 vehicles, supported by 8 floors of multi-level car parking with direct connectivity to all floors of the mall and an intelligent parking guidance system that suit all types of car parks, customer convenience has been prioritized at the mall.

  • The 12-screen PVR superplex will be launched next year, which will include P[XL] and 4 DX screens, premium LUXE auditoriums, and one auditorium dedicated to children, the Playhouse.

  • Spectacular flagship store designs, combined with an abundant offering of food and entertainment, the mall will be a true destination mall for the people of Thiruvananthapuram and beyond.

  • When it launches on 16th December 2021, LuLu Mall Thiruvananthapuram will have a key role to play in the Tourism sector of Kerala, just like its counterpart in Kochi.
