About Mall

LuLu Mall Thiruvananthapuram is a division of the much-famed LuLu Group, headquartered in Abu Dhabi UAE. It is one of the largest as well as the first of its kind in India.Located at Akkulam , on NH 66 bypass, in close proximity to Technopark, LuLu Mall will open its doors for an enchanting international shopping experience.With a built-up space of 185800 sq. m., the mall is spread across ground plus two storeys. The mall has over 300 national and international brands and 12 Anchor Stores including the LuLu Hypermarket spread across 18500 sq. m.Funtura, at 7400 sq. m. is the largest indoor entertainment zone in the country. Its biggest attractions include a nearly 1000 ft long roll glider which circulates the food court, wall climbing for adults and kids, 6 lane bowling alleys, 550 sq. m. trampoline, Tag Arena, 7D theatre with interactive games, roller coaster, arcade games with VR zone, carousel and much more...
